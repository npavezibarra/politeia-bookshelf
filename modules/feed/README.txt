== Description ==

The feed module displays a timeline of user activities and insights.

Current Features:
*   Show Reading Notes written by the user.

Planned Features:
*   **Activity Messages**: Short, tweet-like messages. Integration with BuddyBoss or a custom implementation.
*   **User Stats & Landmarks**: Automatic posts highlighting reading streaks, landmarks (e.g., "100 pages read"), and habit consistency charts.
*   **Global/Community Stats**: "Most read books this week", "Most read genre", "Top book owners".
*   **Distribution Algorithm**: A smart algorithm to determine the mix and frequency of these different post types.
*   **Multiple Templates**: Development of varied design templates to experiment with different feed layouts.
